Privacy Policy

WHOOP Integration -- Angus

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Bridge Intelligence, Inc. ("we", "our", "us") operates the Angus application, which integrates with the WHOOP platform. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect data accessed through the WHOOP API.

Data We Access

When you authorize Angus, we may request access to the following WHOOP data on your behalf:

  • Recovery data (score, heart rate variability, resting heart rate)
  • Cycle data (day strain, average heart rate)
  • Sleep data (duration, start and end times)
  • Workout data (activity type, accumulated strain)
  • Profile information
  • Body measurements (height, weight, max heart rate)

How We Use Your Data

Your WHOOP data is used solely to provide you with personalized health and performance insights within the Angus application. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health data with third parties.

Data Storage and Security

Data retrieved from WHOOP is stored securely and encrypted at rest. We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Revoking Access

You may revoke Angus's access to your WHOOP data at any time through your WHOOP app settings or by contacting us directly. Upon revocation, we will delete all stored WHOOP data associated with your account.

Third-Party Services

This application accesses data through the WHOOP API. Your use of WHOOP is governed by WHOOP's own privacy policy and terms of service.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the application or via email.
